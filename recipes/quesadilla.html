How to make Quesadilla!

This recipe will teach you how to make a delicious Quesadilla!

Ingredients
  • Large Flour Tortillas
  • Grated cheese
  • Olive oil
Steps to make Quesadilla
  1. Heat the tortillas until air pockets form
  2. Add cheese and other ingredients
  3. Lower heat and cover pan
  4. Fold tortilla over
  5. Remove quesadilla from the pan and cut into wedges
